XDOG十亿美金计划基金会 XDOG Billion Dollar Plan Foundation

为支持 X Layer 生态和 $XDOG 社区发展，布仔首批斥资 2100 OKB 成立 $XDOG 社区维稳基金 To support X Layer ecosystem and $XDOG community development, Buzai initially invested 2100 OKB to establish the $XDOG Community Stability Fund

∞

#XDOG10亿美金计划 用途 #XDOG1BillionDollarPlan Purpose

1

在 10000 $XDOG = 0.21 OKB位置 购入 1 亿枚 $XDOG 。

Purchase 100 million $XDOG at the price of 10000 $XDOG = 0.21 OKB.

2

$XDOG 价格每上涨0.21 OKB，维稳基金售出 100万枚 $XDOG (首笔售出价值约21枚OKB)。售出后维稳基金会同步销毁100万枚 $XDOG ，同时售出所得 OKB 保留在维稳基金内作为 $XDOG 价值托底。

For every 0.21 OKB increase in $XDOG price, the stability fund sells 1 million $XDOG (the first sale is worth approximately 21 OKB). After the sale, the stability fund will simultaneously burn 1 million $XDOG, and the OKB obtained from the sale will remain in the stability fund as the value support for $XDOG.

3

维稳基金视情况提高托底价格。

The stability fund may increase the support price depending on the situation.

4

若 $XDOG 价格未触及卖出价格，则所有购得的 $XDOG 永久锁仓。

If the $XDOG price does not reach the selling price, all purchased $XDOG will be locked permanently.

5

若维稳基金完成1亿枚 $XDOG 购买并资金耗尽，则对维稳基金进行再次补充。

If the stability fund completes the purchase of 100 million $XDOG and runs out of funds, the stability fund will be replenished again.

6

本维稳基金计划在 $XDOG 市值达到10亿美金之前有效。

This stability fund plan is valid until the market value of $XDOG reaches 1 billion USD.

7

维稳基金会定期公布回购和销毁记录，实盘地址：0xfd0f7d8a9fc80d5c83241182582aff915cba7fab

The stability foundation will regularly publish repurchase and burn records, real address: 0xfd0f7d8a9fc80d5c83241182582aff915cba7fab
